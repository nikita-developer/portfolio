About us. We cover a wide range of complex issues of forecasting in financial markets (prices, volatility and other parameters), solving which requires sophisticated modelling on large data sets. High level of competition in financial markets demands continuous improvement of trading algorithms and strategies. To stay on top one needs to know about latest trends and publications in science and technology. The kind of tasks we are solving often do not have ready-made solutions by standard methods and require innovation, collaboration, passion and readiness to devote your time to research state of the art papers of professional field.
Our team:
Made of experts in financial mathematics, econometrics and machine learning
Uses various tools: from stochastic differential equations to Bayesian approach to probability and reinforcement machine learning
Has a high degree of freedom in research with low bureaucratic burden
